Data Privacy

This project focused on data privacy. It explored some of the history and politics around online privacy, such as the story of Edward Snowden. It also explains ways for users to improve their online privacy.

Purpose/Intended Audience

Our intended audience are teens and young adults. We tried to streamline the language and content to keep it engaging. We also included elements, like the history timeline of Edward Snowden, that can help capture the younger audience's attention. This site is meant for people to learn more about how to protect their privacy and to have awareness.
