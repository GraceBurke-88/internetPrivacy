Shoshana Zuboff on Surveillance Capitalism

Metadata


Essentially many companies are now just mining for metadata

Examples


-The game Pokemon Go was developed by google, but put out through another brand to appear to be an independent startup
-The game is made to gather metadata and influence users to go to certain places (for example starbucks can pay to have a pokemon location be near their coffee shop)
-Other companies, like smart thermostats and smart cars are making tons of money by selling user metadata

How they use metadata


-This metadata can be used to find out alot about human behavior, for example, someone's political affiliation or sexual orientation
-For example, Supermarkets send out coupons based on metadata about shoppers, since one girl started buying unscented shampoo, this was an indicator of her being pregnant, the store sent coupons to her house for baby formula- the teen girl did not even know she was pregnant yet
